# Chinook SQL Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Chinook music store database using SQL to answer business-related questions about sales, customers, artists, genres, albums, tracks, and playlists.
The objective is to demonstrate SQL skills including joins, aggregations, grouping, sorting, and subqueries while extracting meaningful business insights from the data.

---

## Database

The Chinook database is a sample digital music store database containing information about:

- Customers
- Invoices
- Invoice Items
- Tracks
- Albums
- Artists
- Genres
- Playlists

---

## SQL Skills Demonstrated

- SELECT Statements
- INNER JOIN
- Aggregate Functions (SUM, COUNT)
- GROUP BY
- ORDER BY
- Subqueries
- Data Analysis and Reporting

---

## Business Questions Solved

### 1. Total Revenue Generated
Calculated the total revenue generated by the music store.

### 2. Revenue by Country
Identified countries generating the highest revenue.

### 3. Top Customers by Spending
Found customers who spent the most money.

### 4. Customer Count by Country
Analyzed customer distribution across countries.

### 5. USA Customers by State
Examined customer distribution within U.S. states.

### 6. Most Popular Artist by Revenue
Determined which artist generated the highest revenue.

### 7. Most Popular Genre by Revenue
Identified the highest revenue-generating music genre.

### 8. Top Selling Track by Revenue
Found the track that generated the most revenue.

### 9. Top Albums by Revenue
Identified the highest revenue-generating albums.

### 10. Tracks per Playlist
Counted the number of tracks in each playlist.

### 11. Playlists per Track
Determined how many playlists each track appears in.
